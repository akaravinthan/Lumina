About Us

Welcome to "Unsung Hero's," a website dedicated to teaching children about India's brave freedom leaders.

Our mission is to make history fun, engaging, and easy to understand for young minds. Through colorful stories, illustrations, and interactive content, kids can learn about the courage, sacrifice, and inspiring journeys of heroes like Mahatma Gandhi, Rani Lakshmibai, and many more.

We believe every child should grow up knowing the values of bravery, equality, and leadership, and our website provides a safe, creative space to explore these stories.
